# Introduction

Hello! This is Rarick, a Unity developer. I have developed some mini-programs and small games, and I am also planning new games. I hope to officially participate in a Game Jam in the future!  
Game Jam is one of the events I most want to participate in. It not only challenges the limits of game development but also affirms my own abilities. No matter what the final outcome is, it represents my high-intensity involvement in something I love, and the result of that labor. (Of course, if it's my first time participating, I'll probably be completely confused XD)

Currently, I am primarily active in the Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Shanghai regions. I often visit places like comic Fes and Jams. If you're interested, come find me and let's hang out!

## Education

I graduated from Jinling Institute of Technology, an unassuming and relatively unknown university in Nanjing that enriched my life.

![img](./collage-logo.png)

Everyone at the university was hardworking and diligent. Whatever they were involved in, they strived for professionalism and excellence. The university is quite large for its area in the university town—it takes 15 minutes to walk from the south end to the north end (there weren't even shuttle buses, so we had to walk). In the Computer Science department, my roommates and classmates helped each other, shared knowledge, and solved problems together. The time spent pulling all-night to write reports and repeatedly reviewing papers was exhausting, but it was also the most unforgettable part of my university life.

By the way, I encountered the COVID-19 during my university years, which led to a long lockdown. 😢 Fortunately, the lockdown was lifted before graduation, but then I had to prepare for my thesis (such is life!). I had originally planned to participate in the Gibit University Development Competition, but I missed it because of writing my thesis... (I'm so sorry, my teammates!)

## Hobbies and Interests

Shocking! A developer who actually enjoys going out and taking photos! (What a twist!)

Yes, you heard it right—I really enjoy photography. A few of my photos are archived on [LFI](https://lfi-online.de/en/gallery/Rarick-Wong/Glowing-Light-1959628.html) (though I didn't win any awards, because I'm too lazy to post-process them -- the raw images are already good enough for me).

My photography gear is quite simple: just a camera, and maybe a spot light at most. My camera lens specifications are:
- SummiLux 1:1.7/28mm ASPH.
- F1.7
- Max shutter speed: 1/36000s
- Focal lengths: 28/35/50mm

Additionally, I enjoy playing the tin whistle (and I also want to learn the flute—I plan to find a teacher at a music studio in the future). Although my breath control isn't perfect yet, I can basically play any tune I want. I also like to dabble with FL Studio as a hobby. I'm currently studying music theory and have completed the basics (elementary to high school level).

Through studying music theory, I realized that the barrier to creation isn't the tools (professionals use Logic Pro), but rather a solid foundation and rich creativity. The biggest difference between humans and AI is that humans possess an unparalleled imagination that AI cannot match. It is this imagination that drives the continuous progress of human society and living standards.
